FAQ

What is MentorKart®?

MentorKart® is the next gen 1:1 mentoring platform in India bringing together renowned mentors and mentees who need mentoring.

How does MentorKart® assign mentors?

MentorKart® has intelligent algorithms for matching a mentee with a relevant and highly rated mentors. So you can be assured of getting the best.

How does MentorKart® work?

Sign up on MentorKart® as a mentee and set your professional and personal goals. You can set up to 6 goals. Choose a membership option and a relevant mentor is assigned to you. Take up the mentorship program with the mentor and whoosh achieve your goals.

I already have a mentor assigned at workplace, Why should I sign up at MentorKart®?

Mentoring at workplace would prepare you for the next level role in the organizational hierarchy whereas mentoring program with MentorKart® would give you access to the best industry mentors with some exclusive webinars, articles and book excerpts widening your scope of learning.

How does MentorKart® ensures the quality of the program?

All the mentoring programs are being closely monitored by our panel of observers who ensure the quality of programs and also assist in recalibration if the mentee needs assistance.

What are the goal-based 1:1 Mentorship programs?

In this program, there are various mentors present who help you to achieve your goals through 1:1 mentorship sessions. It happens with a Mentorship Model Framework where you set specific goals, create a concrete plan with your Mentor, and then achieve them.

Are the 1:1 Goal-Based Mentorship Packages only for Professionals?

Mentorkart 1:1 Goal-Based Mentorship is not only for professionals but also for students and entrepreneurs as well. If you have goals and you want to achieve them then we have 1:1 Goal-Based Mentorship.

What type of Programs & Courses do you have on the Platform?

Whether you are a Professional, student, or entrepreneur, we have various programs starting from cracking Job Interviews to career growth. Also, there are various programs to achieve your start-up goals as well.

Are the Programs & Courses free or paid?

There are various programs that are present which are in the form of recorded content on the platform that you can watch absolutely free. However, we have some paid programs and courses as well. You can visit the platform and check out as per your requirements.

What is the Job Guarantee Program?

The program is specifically designed for various job roles. In this program; firstly, you set your goal through 1:1 mentorship then there are 200+ hours of interactive live training with industry mentors, next there is screening before your interviews and finally, you get full placement assistance from us till you get placed. You get unlimited interviews until you get placed in the company.

What are the chances for me to get a Job in Job Guarantee Program?

We assure you a 100% guaranteed job. We provide you with 200+ hours of live interactive industrial training where you learn the industry-required skill. A nd then you get a full placement assistance from us where you get premium job referrals and unlimited job interviews till you get placed. Once you get through all of these then it becomes obvious for you to get placed. We trust our process and that’s why we assure you of a 100% guaranteed job.

How Active Learning Subscription can help me in my career?

With this subscription, you get access to attend any live session. You can attend various live sessions related to specific jobs, cracking interviews and career growth, and many more. Based on your job preferences you c an attend all the possible live sessions to shine your career.

What do I get in the Active Learning Subscription?

You get Live Session related to 5 Industry Job Roles, 15+ hours of Job-Specific Live Sessions, multiple live-sessions in Soft Skills also motivational videos by Ashish Vidyarthi.

How to attend your Live Sessions?

To attend the live sessions you can log in to Mentorkart App and then all the upcoming live sessions will be visible to you. There are various live sessions we conduct that you can attend for FREE. Also, there are many recorded videos present on the platform that you can access for FREE and learn from them.
